# 🎩 Gorrito Learning

Welcome to **Gorrito Learning**, the official open-source sandbox and learning laboratory for **@gorritocodes**. 

I’m building this organization to document my journey from a capable JavaScript/TypeScript developer to a tech entrepreneur. Right now, I'm 33 years old. I know where I want to go, and I know exactly what I need to learn to get there. I’m building and breaking things in public because, in a couple of years, no one is going to believe I started here. 🚀

---

## 🎯 The Mission
To master the full-stack JS/TS ecosystem, transition away from AI-reliant styling, learn professional DevOps/Architecture, and build production-ready SaaS templates. This organization holds the receipts of that evolution.

---

## 🗺️ The Learning Roadmap

Here is what I am actively building projects to master. You can track my progress across these core pillars:

### 🎨 Frontend & UI Mastery
*   **CSS & Styling:** Moving away from AI-generated styles to master responsive layout, modern CSS, and component libraries.
*   **React & Next.js:** Deepening my understanding of Next.js routing, server components, and performance optimizations.
*   **Expo:** Building cross-platform mobile experiences with confidence.

### 🏗️ Backend, Architecture & Databases
*   **TypeScript Generics:** Moving past basic types to master complex, reusable generic types.
*   **Node.js, Express & NestJS:** Elevating from basic servers to enterprise-grade, middleware-heavy NestJS architectures.
*   **Monorepos:** Perfecting the **Turborepo** monolith workflow.
*   **Databases & Tooling:** Transitioning from MongoDB-only to mastering SQL, **Prisma ORM**, and **Zod** for schema validation.
*   **Auth:** Implementing professional authentication via **Clerk**.

### 🚀 DevOps, Git & Quality Assurance (The "Pro" Layer)
*   **Git & Workflow:** Mastering semantic commits, advanced branching strategies, and **Husky** hooks.
*   **CI/CD:** Building automated pipelines using **GitHub Actions**.
*   **Containerization:** Learning **Docker** for professional local development and deployments.
*   **Testing:** Expanding my **Jest** foundation into end-to-end testing with **Playwright**.
